A bit about the project

COVID-19 and its consequences are still a bit of a touchy subject. Nonetheless, the virus provides a lot of interesting information which could be used to better understand and defend against the disease.

The point of this project was to get a unique insight of the situation and as a result find correlations between different metrics of the countries and COVID-19 related deaths.
